Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1/1/2023

Last Updated: 4/12/2025

atau.io ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how our Excel macros interact with your Gmail and Google Sheets data and what safeguards are in place to protect your privacy.

1. What We Access

With your authorization, our macros access the following services through Google APIs:

  • Gmail API: Only to find and download attachments from specific Paytronix emails.
  • Google Sheets API: To upload or download store inventory, HACCP logs, and store orders.

2. What We Don’t Do

  • We do not store, view, or access any user data remotely.
  • We do not transmit any user data to our servers.
  • We do not analyze, sell, or share your Gmail or Google Sheets data.

All data usage is entirely local to your device and only for the intended macro functions. Temporary files (e.g., ZIP attachments) are automatically deleted from your machine after processing.

3. Purpose of Access

Our macros are designed to:

  • Keep store inventory data in sync between Excel and Google Sheets.
  • Power an AppSheet application using this synced data.
  • Retrieve, extract, and compare sales data from Paytronix ZIP files found in your Gmail inbox.

4. Data Handling and Storage

  • No personal data is stored by us.
  • No Gmail messages are saved or viewed—only ZIP attachments are processed locally.
  • Google Sheets access is limited to documents you explicitly authorize.

5. Compliance with Google Policies

Our application complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements.

6. Consent and Revocation

Your consent is granted through Google's OAuth screen. You can revoke access at any time in your Google account settings.
